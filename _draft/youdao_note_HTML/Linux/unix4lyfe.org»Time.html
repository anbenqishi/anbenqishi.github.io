Sometime-relatedconsiderationswhenprogramming:

Mostofyourcodeshouldn'tbedealingwithtimezonesorlocaltime,itshouldbepassingUnix

formatting. This stuff is tricky, avoid re-implementing it yourself.
Whenmeasuringanintervaloftime,useamonotonic(alwaysincreasing)clock.Read

mostlyvarieswithtemperature.

correcttime.
Slewispreferredbecauseit'slessdisruptive,butit'sonlyusefulforcorrectingsmalloffsets.
